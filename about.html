Mission & Values

Mission: Our mission is to provide customers with high quality products from a secure supply chain, to minimise business disruption. Our directors and staff strive to deliver performance and quality to always delight our customers.

Values: Success in our future depends on the integrity and ethical behaviour of all our employees. We support and nourish our staff to improve and grow as responsible members of a civil society. We take personal responsibility for our actions and respect others with different views, as we continually strive for improvements for our customers, our company and ourselves.

Why Choose Us

Sinext Pty Ltd is a Melbourne-based Australian-Chinese trading company that has been exporting mainly Australian products to China since 2014.

We are a privately-owned company registered in Australia. Each of Sinext’s directors has more than 15 years experience in supplying products and services into China. We have a detailed knowledge and understanding of the Chinese market and regulatory environment.
